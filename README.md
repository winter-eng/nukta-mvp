# NUKTA: Safe & Share 🚀
**The "Airbnb for Retail Shelves" – Transforming urban spaces into a decentralized marketplace.**

## 🌍 Overview
NUKTA is a hyper-local e-commerce ecosystem designed to empower small businesses and local creators. We provide a platform where any business owner (cafes, pharmacies, etc.) can rent out empty shelf space to local sellers, creating automated self-service micro-points across the city.

## ✨ Key Features
*   **Hyper-Local Commerce:** Buy and sell goods within your neighborhood without high rent or shipping costs.
*   **Triple-Trust Protocol:**
    *   **KYC Verification:** All sellers are verified to ensure community safety.
    *   **AI Expiry Control:** Smart scanning technology to prevent the sale of expired goods.
    *   **Safe Seal System:** Physical Host verification to ensure product integrity.
*   **Host Arbitration:** Business owners act as real-world mediators for secure P2P transactions.
*   **Anti-Fraud System:** POS-linked video verification for disputes.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
*   **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion (Mobile-first PWA).
*   **Backend:** Python (FastAPI), SQLite.
*   **Logic:** Smart trust-score algorithms & OCR integration.
